Perfect Routine & Diet Plan for Lumbar Scoliosis

A structured routine to improve posture and reduce discomfort.

"My power isn’t in my back... It’s in my unbreakable spirit!" – Naruto Uzumaki

🕒 Daily Routine

🔹 Morning (10-15 min) – Stretching & Warm-up
🔹 Afternoon (15-20 min) – Core Strengthening
🔹 Evening (10 min) – Relaxation & Posture Fixing

🧘‍♂️ Morning Routine (15 min)

✅ Goal: Increase flexibility & reduce stiffness

1. Side Stretch (Left) – 3 Sets (20 sec)
2. Cat-Cow Stretch – 10 Reps
3. Cobra Stretch – 3 Sets (20 sec)
4. Child’s Pose – 30 sec Hold

🏋️ Afternoon Routine (20 min)

✅ Goal: Strengthen core & stabilize spine

1. Pelvic Tilts – 3 Sets (10 Reps)
2. Bridge Pose – 3 Sets (10 Reps)
3. Side Plank (Left) – 3 Sets (30 sec)
4. Resistance Band Pulls – 10 Reps

🌙 Evening Routine (10 min)

✅ Goal: Fix posture, relax muscles

1. Wall Posture Hold – 3 Sets (30 sec)
2. Deep Breathing – 5 min

🥗 Diet Plan for Spinal Health

✅ Focus on: Anti-inflammatory foods & bone health

🔹 Breakfast: Whole grain toast + Avocado + Nuts
🔹 Snack: Fruits (Banana, Berries) + Almonds
🔹 Lunch: Brown Rice + Dal + Veggies (Rice 2 days/week)
🔹 Snack: Cottage Cheese + Dry Fruits
🔹 Dinner: Veggie Soup + Whole Wheat Roti

❌ Foods to Avoid:

🚫 Too much sugar, Processed food, Soda

🎯 Additional Tips

✔ Hydrate well (2-3L water daily)
✔ Correct sitting posture
✔ Use a firm mattress
